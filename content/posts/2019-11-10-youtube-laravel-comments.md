This is the first time that I know of, that someone has published a video about one of my packages.
After verifying the link and seeing the video I was very happy and proud.
The author of the video ([Andre Madarang](https://andremadarang.com/)) explained the package, demonstrated how to use it, how to customize it,
and he even built a simple Vue.js component for displaying and posting comments with this package.

I've included the link to the video in the package [readme file](https://github.com/laravelista/comments#tutorials--articles) so that everyone can benefit from it.

You can find the links bellow:

- [YouTube: Laravel Comments](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YhA0CSX1HIg)  by Andre Madarang
- [Package: laravelista/comments](https://github.com/laravelista/comments)
