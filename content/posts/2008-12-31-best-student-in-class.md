I’m writing this in retrospect, to the best of my memory (19.7.2026).

This was the first time I was rewarded with the title of being the best at something.
At the time, it didn’t mean much to me, because I was focused on other things.
Still, I remember feeling proud, especially because it was my first year at university.
I had moved towns and was living alone for the first time in my life.

A lot had to go right for this to happen. I was lucky to have made good friends, and to find interest in the subjects I was studying.
There were courses where I received praise and recognition from my professors, and that meant a great deal to me.

I absorbed as much as I could during that period, and I pushed myself even harder.
